What is technical SEO?

Technical SEO is a specialized part of SEO which focuses primarily on ensuring that your website can be easily "crawlable" by modern search engines. This usually involves making sure the code structure is easy to read, meta tags are implemented, links are optimized, keywords are implemented, and more. SEO is an extremely broad field with a lot of moving parts. For us, focusing on this one integral part of SEO optimization allows us to build an optimized website that meets our quality standards instead of being stretched too thin.
