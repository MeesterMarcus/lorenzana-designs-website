Why use WordPress?

We help provide San Antonio businesses with websites using industry leading technologies. One of the most popular and proven web design platforms is WordPress. See why it's so widely used and why it makes sense for your business.

Easy to customize

One of the great things about the WordPress CMS platform is that it allows for easy customization and configuration. WordPress also comes with a wide array of themes to choose from that have been proven to appeal to viewers and enable better interaction.

SEO Friendly

WordPress websites have a high probability of ranking well in Google since it has a lot of built in SEO features that allow web designers to optimize their content. In addition to that, there are a number of plugins that enhance the SEO toolbox even further that have been maintained and improved upon over the years.

Most popular CMS

WordPress is the most popular Content Management System in the world. I know what your thinking... this isn't a popularity contest! But when it comes to building websites it is extremely important because it gives you access to a large community that has done just about any type of site you can imagine in WordPress. Having that level of support and security knowing many popular website are built with WordPress and have been around for ages is comforting to know. There is a reason why it is so popular. It's very productive for developers and easy to use for clients to manage their content and posts.

Lower Maintenance Cost

WordPress requires less setup and maintenance compared to other open source CMS's such as Drupal or Joomla. It also is great particularly for teams and future development. A large majority of issues can be resolved just by going through the admin interface and you can see your changes as your are editing them. Since WordPress has such a large community as well, bringing on other people to maintain your website will be simpler.
